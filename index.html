Questions

Asked before every hire.

Is Tommy a real AI or a call center?
Real AI, trained on your store — no offshore call center, no script readers. Tommy answers from your actual inventory, pricing, and calendar. And when something needs a human, Tommy brings it to your team, not to a stranger in a cubicle.
What happens when Tommy doesn't know an answer?
Tommy says so, and hands the conversation to your team immediately — with the full transcript attached so you're not starting from zero. Tommy never guesses at a payment, a trade-in number, or a policy. You'd fire a BDC rep who winged it. So would we.
Will customers know it's not human?
If they ask, Tommy tells them the truth: it's a virtual assistant. We don't pretend, and we don't need to — car buyers care about getting an answer in 60 seconds at 11pm, not who typed it. Most just say thanks and book the test drive.
What do you need from me to get started?
About an hour, total. Your inventory feed, your service menu, your hours, your calendar access, and how you like to talk to customers. We handle the rest during the 14-day onboarding — you keep running your store.
Is this compliant? STOP opt-outs, quiet hours?
Yes, out of the box. The sender ID identifies your dealership, every customer can reply STOP to opt out instantly, and Tommy respects quiet hours and consent windows. We handle carrier registration and keep an auditable log of every message and consent.
What about customers on Android?
Nobody slips through. iPhone customers get real iMessage — blue bubbles, typing indicators. Android customers get SMS from the same number, same speed, same Tommy. The conversation looks right on every phone.
What if I already have a BDC?
Keep it. Your BDC works 40 hours a week. Tommy covers the other 128 — nights, weekends, holidays, and every lead that comes in while your reps are already on the phone. Tommy makes your BDC bigger, not smaller.
